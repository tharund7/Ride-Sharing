Welcome to KSU Ride Sharing

KSU Ride Sharing is a platform that allows students to share rides with each other.
It is a great way to save money and reduce the number of cars on the road. It is also a great way to meet new people and make new friends.
If you are a driver, you can offer rides to other students and make some extra money.
If you are a rider, you can find rides to get to your destination.
It is a win-win for everyone!
